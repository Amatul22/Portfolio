A little bit about my world

I'm an undergraduate at North South University, finishing up a degree in Computer Science & Engineering - but honestly, my heart lives in the in-between spaces: writing captions that make people smile, organizing events that bring people together, and chasing good light for a photograph.

I'm fluent in Bengali and English, comfortable across Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, Filmora, and Canva, and I'm endlessly curious about how creativity and structure can sit in the same sentence. My long-term hope is to build a career in academia - somewhere I can keep learning out loud and help others do the same.
